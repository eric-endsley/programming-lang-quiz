Sounds like you're a virus! Javascript is the best language for viruses, because learning javascript is simply infectious! It may be tough since you aren't technically a living thing, but since you managed to fill out this quiz, I'm sure you can do it!

Most single-celled amoebas such as yourself love to learn C#. Did you know C# was actually invented by an amoeba? Me neither, but for all anyone knows, it's true. Put those pseudopods to work and start coding!

Ruby!

This may seem unusual for a simple multi-cellular algae like you, but Ruby is the coding language you can't afford not to learn. It's just as colorfully named as one of your many varieties, and there are surely photosynthesis enhancing web applications just waiting to be developed!
